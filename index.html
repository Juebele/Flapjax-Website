Flapjax is the brainchild of culinary enthusiast Alex Barlowe. Passionate about the art of pancake-making, Alex has spent years in the kitchen perfecting a medley of flapjack recipes, ranging from classic to creatively whimsical. Launched in 2020, Flapjax aims to transform ordinary events into memorable feasts with a delicious assortment of pancakes and beloved breakfast staples.

Our versatile menu is tailored to cater to any occasion - be it a tailgate, a corporate brunch, or a birthday celebration. Our team ensures that each pancake is made to perfection, bringing joy and flavor to every bite. Ready to add a twist to your next event? Ask us how Flapjax can sprinkle a dash of delicious magic to make it unforgettable!
