About Flapjax

Contact Us

Flapjax is the brainchild of our founder Alex Barlowe. Passionate about perfecting breakfast, Alex would often find himself in the kitchen experimenting on a variety of creative recipes. From classic to whimsical, Alex perfected a medley of fun pancakes that he would share with friends. In 2020, Alex decided to turn his passion into a business and Flapjax was born! At Flapjax, we combine quality ingredients, a fearless spirit, and love for our neighbors to bring you an unforgettable breakfast experience!
